## A. Origin (1967–1988)

### 1967-08-19 — Born in Hyderabad

- Telugu Hindu family in Hyderabad, Andhra Pradesh (now Telangana), India.
- Father: Bukkapuram Nadella Yugandhar, Indian Administrative Service (1962 batch); later worked in the Indian PMO.
- Mother: Prabhavati, Sanskrit lecturer.
- Source (composite): https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella ; https://www.britannica.com/money/Satya-Nadella ; https://news.microsoft.com/source/exec/satya-nadella/

### ~1978–1984 — Hyderabad Public School (HPS), Begumpet

- One of India's most well-known boys' boarding schools.
- Played cricket, football, and competed in debate; cricket became a lifelong frame for teamwork (cited in *Hit Refresh* and the Dreamcricket leadership review https://www.dreamcricket.com/articles/usa-cricket-news/crickets-lessons-in-leadership--a-review-of-hit-refresh-by-satya-nadella/ ).
- Source: composite via Wikipedia and Global Indian profile https://www.globalindian.com/profiles/satya-nadella/

### 1988 — B.Tech, Electrical Engineering, Manipal Institute of Technology

- Manipal Institute (then under Mangalore University, now Manipal University).
- After being denied admission to IIT (his first-choice), Nadella enrolled at Manipal.
- The school later named a building / wing after him.

### 1988 — Emigrated to the US on student visa

- Story documented at https://www.tryalma.com/learn/satya-nadella-immigration-story (third-party recap).

## B. Graduate School and Early Career (1988–1992)

### 1990 — M.S. in Computer Science, University of Wisconsin–Milwaukee

- Two-year program; switched from electrical engineering to computer science.
- Source: https://en.wikipedia.org/wiki/Satya_Nadella ; Britannica.

### 1990 — Joined Sun Microsystems

- Member of technology staff; brief stint of roughly two years.

### 1992 — Joined Microsoft

- Initial role: Windows Developer Relations.
- Worked on Windows NT, the line that became Microsoft's enterprise server OS spine.
- Source: Microsoft official bio https://news.microsoft.com/source/exec/satya-nadella/ (403 to WebFetch; cross-confirmed via https://www.britannica.com/money/Satya-Nadella ).

## C. Microsoft Rise (1992–2014)

### 1992–1999 — Windows Developer Relations / Windows NT / Internet Explorer / SQL Server era

- Multiple product-management roles inside the Server & Tools Business under Bob Muglia and later Eric Rudder.

### 1997 — MBA, University of Chicago Booth (executive program, while at Microsoft)

- Source: composite Britannica + Wikipedia.

### 1999 — Married Anupama (Anu) Venugopal

- A classmate's daughter from Hyderabad; their families had known each other for decades. The marriage is referenced extensively in *Hit Refresh*.

### 2000s — Microsoft Business Solutions (Dynamics)

- Senior VP of MBS (Dynamics ERP/CRM), a role that gave him deep ToB / enterprise software experience that would later prove load-bearing for Azure positioning.

### 2007 — VP of Microsoft Business Division (Business Solutions)

### 2008–2011 — Senior VP, R&D for the Online Services Division (Bing)

- Ran the Bing engineering organization during the search-engine pivot vs. Google. Bing was unprofitable for years but built the search-ranking / data-center stack that later supported Azure.

### 2011-02 — Promoted to President, Server & Tools Business (STB)

- Replaced Bob Muglia.
- Took over Windows Server, SQL Server, Visual Studio, Microsoft Azure (then called "Windows Azure," launched 2010).
- This is the role in which he became the architect of Microsoft's cloud bet.

### 2013-07 — Reorganization: Cloud & Enterprise EVP

- Steve Ballmer's "One Microsoft" reorganization renamed STB → Cloud and Enterprise.
- Nadella's group became the strategic center of gravity for Microsoft's future.

## E. Personal Life (lightly anchored, public material only)

- Wife: Anu Nadella. Three children. Eldest son Zain born with cerebral palsy in 1996; Zain died in 2022. Nadella has spoken extensively in *Hit Refresh* about how raising Zain shaped his understanding of empathy.
- Cricket: lifelong devotion; built a cricket oval on Microsoft's Redmond campus. https://qz.com/work/1153042/microsofts-ceo-satya-nadella-is-building-a-proper-cricket-oval-at-its-us-campus
- Books cited by him as formative (per *Hit Refresh* + Stanford GSB):
  - Carol Dweck, *Mindset: The New Psychology of Success* — origin of "growth mindset" / "learn-it-all" language.
  - René Girard's works on mimetic desire (cited in *Hit Refresh*).
  - Tracy Kidder, *Mountains Beyond Mountains* — empathy/global-health frame.
  - Joseph O'Neill, *Netherland* — cricket-as-team metaphor.
- Boards / civic roles over time: Starbucks (2017–2024, exit 2024), University of Chicago Booth advisory, Fred Hutchinson Cancer Research Center.
