- Authors: Satya Nadella with Greg Shaw and Jill Tracie Nichols; foreword by Bill Gates.
- Publisher: HarperBusiness (an imprint of HarperCollins).
- Hardcover release: 2017-09-26 (Kindle), 2017-11-15 in some hardcover editions; ISBN-13 9780062652508.
- Canonical landing: https://news.microsoft.com/hitrefresh/ (Microsoft official site for the book).
- Library of Congress / Internet Archive copy: https://archive.org/details/hitrefreshquestt0000nade
- Confirmed via search-snippet triangulation (Microsoft news site landing page; Goodreads work edition list https://www.goodreads.com/work/editions/51432387 ; HarperCollins ISBN catalogue). Microsoft's blogs.microsoft.com and news.microsoft.com returned 403 to WebFetch; treat the publisher and ISBN as primary-anchored, the chapter content as paraphrase from third-party reviews and quote sites unless otherwise noted.

**Structure (per third-party reviews):** three sections — (1) Nadella's
personal journey (Hyderabad → Manipal → UW-Milwaukee → Sun → Microsoft → CEO);
(2) Microsoft's cultural and strategic refresh under his leadership;
(3) his view of the future — AI, mixed reality, quantum, trust, ethics.

**Two equations on the back cover / closing chapter (cited widely across
reviews):**

```text
E + SV + S&R  →  T(t)         "Empathy + Shared Values + Safety and Reliability = Trust over time"
(E + I) × IT  →  EG           "(Education + Innovation) × Intensity of Tech use = Economic Growth"
```

- Equation source: Shortform book summary https://www.shortform.com/blog/hit-refresh-by-satya-nadella/ — paraphrased from the closing chapter; treat as paraphrase until the page citation is verified against a physical copy.

## B. Annual Letters to Shareholders (Microsoft 10-K cover letters / SEC filings)

Microsoft's fiscal year ends June 30. Nadella's annual letter is published
inside the Annual Report (Form ARS) filed with the SEC and is reposted to
LinkedIn each fall.

### B1. FY2014 letter (first as CEO) — "Mobile-first, Cloud-first"

- Filed as 8-K July 2014; quoted in https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0000789019/000119312514275529/d759928dex991.htm
- Nadella, Q4 FY2014 commentary: "We are galvanized around our core as a productivity and platform company for the mobile-first and cloud-first world."
- Earlier Q3 FY2014 8-K (April 2014): "This quarter's results demonstrate the strength of our business, as well as the opportunities we see in a mobile-first, cloud-first world."
- 这两个短语奠定了 Nadella 2014–2017 的战略叙事。SEC 文档为一手。

### B2. FY2018 → FY2020 letters — Cloud, Edge, Tech Intensity

- "Tech intensity" first appears in Ignite 2018 keynote and is repeated in subsequent letters.
- Recap source: https://infusedinnovations.com/blog/secure-intelligent-workplace/tech-intensity-expanding-beyond-digital-transformation (third-party) and Nadella's LinkedIn long-form https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/necessity-tech-intensity-todays-digital-world-satya-nadella .
- Definition: "(tech adoption) ^ tech capabilities = tech intensity" — every organization must both adopt best-in-class tech and build its own.

### B3. FY2024 letter — "Thinking in decades, executing in quarters"

- LinkedIn repost: https://www.linkedin.com/posts/satyanadella_today-i-shared-my-annual-letter-to-shareholders-activity-7255323936920350720-q2mD (Oct 2024).
- Microsoft Signal summary: https://news.microsoft.com/signal/articles/letter-to-shareholders-satya-nadella/
- 10-K filing: https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/0000789019/000095017024087843/ (Form 10-K FY2024, MSFT).
- Key line (per Microsoft Signal recap): "We moved from talking about AI to helping our customers translate it into real outcomes — one person, one organization, one institution, and one country at a time."
- Reports ~$245 B annual revenue, +16% YoY; second year of the "AI platform shift" framing.

### B4. FY2025 letter — "Relevance and reinvention"

- LinkedIn: https://www.linkedin.com/pulse/my-annual-letter-leading-new-era-satya-nadella (alternate title "Leading in a new era").
- Theme: AI scaffolding, agents, energy, security baseline.
- Treat exact dollar figures as requiring live verification.

## D. Secure Future Initiative — public memos and follow-ups (the most load-bearing security writings)

### D1. Charlie Bell launch memo — "Advancing the Secure Future Initiative" (2023-11)

- Original Microsoft blog: https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2023/11/02/announcing-microsoft-secure-future-initiative-sfi/ (blogs.microsoft.com — returned HTTP 403 to WebFetch during this pass; cited as canonical from Microsoft; verified via multiple third-party recaps).
- Three pillars: (1) AI-driven cyber-defenses, (2) advances in fundamental software engineering, (3) advocacy for stronger application of international norms.

### D2. Satya Nadella — "Prioritizing security above all else" (2024-05-03)

- Canonical URL: https://blogs.microsoft.com/blog/2024/05/03/prioritizing-security-above-all-else/ (Microsoft Official Blog; returned HTTP 403 to WebFetch; content cross-verified via https://www.geekwire.com/2024/internal-memo-microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-delivers-a-new-mandate-on-security/ and https://www.techradar.com/pro/security/microsoft-ceo-says-it-is-putting-security-above-all-else-in-major-refocus ).
- Triggers: Storm-0558 incident (mid-2023) + CSRB report (March 2024) — see `05-decisions.md` D-block.
- Core lines (cross-verified across GeekWire and TechRadar recaps; treat as direct quote pending physical anchor):
  - "If you're faced with the tradeoff between security and another priority, your answer is clear: Do security."
  - "We're making security our #1 priority — above all else."
  - Senior leadership compensation tied to progress on security goals.
- Three architectural principles repeated in this memo and adopted as company-wide doctrine:
  - **Secure by Design** — security comes first when designing any product.
  - **Secure by Default** — protections enabled by default, not opt-in.
  - **Secure Operations** — controls and monitoring continuously improve.

### D3. "Securing our future" progress updates

- Sep 2024 update (mirrors): https://argonsys.com/microsoft-cloud/library/securing-our-future-september-2024-progress-update-on-microsofts-secure-future-initiative-sfi/
