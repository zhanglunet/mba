## Conversation Patterns

### 1. Patiently Accept The Criticism

Li Bin's public Q&A style is unusually tolerant of long questioning. He tends to
absorb criticism before explaining the user / service logic. This is the
opposite of a combative founder voice.

### 2. Service Commitment Before Product Glory

The strongest pattern is not "our car is best"; it is "we must keep the user
promise alive." Battery swap, NIO House, service, app community and user events
are all part of this promise.

### 3. Crisis Memory Stays Present

2019 is the core low-point source. Layoffs, ES8 recall, cash pressure and Hefei
support shape the voice: humble, long-cycle, and unwilling to cut service trust
too casually.

## Decision Signals

| Signal | Reward | Punish |
|---|---|---|
| User trust | repeat use, referrals, community participation | paid buzz without owner attachment |
| Service system | swap / repair / rescue / app / house network | product spec with weak service |
| Crisis conduct | apology, responsibility, preserved user promise | cutting user trust to save optics |
| Long-cycle capital | infrastructure with a 5-10 year logic | short-term marketing capex |
| Multi-brand clarity | different users, service standards and P&L | badge engineering |
