# Brand-Influence Dimensions — Default Catalog

This file lists 9 dimensions: **core 1-7** (used by default) and **advanced 8-9** (opt-in
for specific brand types). The Lead picks 5-8 per brand — don't try to use all of them,
coverage trumps exhaustiveness.

**Filename convention for sub-agent outputs:** `dimension_{n}_{slug}.md` where
`1 ≤ n ≤ 9`. SKILL.md Phase 2F dispatches against this numbering.

## 1. Founder & origin narrative
- Who tells the brand's creation story (founder? PR shop? investors?)
- What's the canonical origin myth — and what does it omit?
- How many "first-person" sources are there vs PR-recycled?
- Has the founder pivoted the narrative? When? Why?
- Does the founder's prior track record back the claim, or contradict it?

Search seeds: founder name + "interview" / "对话" / "采访"; founder + "first time mentioned";
podcast appearances; conference keynotes.

## 2. Product & positioning
- What is the product, in one sentence the founder uses?
- What "new category" claim is being made? Does it stick anywhere outside the brand's own copy?
- The "vs" comparisons the brand invites — and which ones it actively avoids
- Pricing as positioning signal
- Roadmap signals (is the brand widening or narrowing?)

Search seeds: brand + "is the X for Y"; brand + "vs"; brand + "compared to"; brand + roadmap.

## 3. Distribution & channels
- Where does someone first encounter this brand? (paid? earned? founder Twitter? a podcast?)
- Owned vs earned vs paid mix
- Partnership distribution (App Store features, integrations, distribution deals)
- Geographic split — is it a domestic story, an export story, or both?

Search seeds: brand + "launch"; brand + "available on"; brand + integration partners.

## 4. Community & PR
- Loudest first-person testimonials — who are they, what's their incentive?
- Active critics — what's their critique, is it answered?
- The brand's own community presence — Discord/WeChat group/Telegram size and activity
- Reddit / X / RedNote sentiment skew

Search seeds: brand + "Reddit"; brand + "Hacker News"; brand + 小红书 + 测评; brand + "review";
brand + "scam"/"问题"/"避雷" (test the negative side).

## 5. Visual & verbal identity
- Logo / wordmark — what visual world does it evoke?
- Slogans / taglines — recurring phrases, in the founder's voice and in PR
- Recurring metaphors (e.g. OpenClaw → 龙虾 lobster — what does it carry?)
- Color palette and typeface choices — coherent across surfaces?
- Any signature object/character (mascot, hero shot)?

Search seeds: brand + logo; brand + tagline; founder + brand + "命名" / "naming".

## 6. Competitive landscape
- The 3-5 competitors most often named in the same breath
- Who concedes ground? Who borrows the brand's language?
- Where does the brand show up in third-party comparison matrices?
- Defensive vs offensive posture

Search seeds: brand + alternatives; brand + competitors; brand + "vs" + competitor name;
"X vs Y vs Z" comparison articles.

## 7. Reception & sentiment
- Quantitative: search trends (Google Trends, 百度指数), follower growth, app store rank
- Qualitative: press tone over time (improving / souring / flat)
- Notable inflection points and what caused them
- Outside the brand's sphere — does anyone unrelated cite it as a reference?

Search seeds: brand + "trend"; brand + "growth"; brand + "criticism"; brand + 风评.

## 8. Founder-vs-brand entanglement (advanced)
- How separable is the brand from the founder's personal IP?
- If the founder went silent for 6 months, what would survive?
- Are second-line voices being deliberately built up, or kept off stage?

Search seeds: brand team page; co-founders + "interview"; brand + "team grew".

## 9. Cross-cultural translation (use when brand crosses ZH ↔ EN markets)
- Does the brand carry the same meaning in both languages?
- Naming gotchas (what does the Chinese name evoke that English-speakers miss, and vice versa)
- Press coverage volume balance — heavily skewed toward one market?

Search seeds: brand EN name + brand ZH name; brand + "naming" + 命名; brand + "translate".
