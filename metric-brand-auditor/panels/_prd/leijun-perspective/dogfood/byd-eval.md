## Distinctiveness Check

### Versus Jobs

The review does not center taste, object simplification, or visual coherence.
It centers manufacturing proof, service, owner trust, premium touchpoints, and
post-purchase confidence. This is meaningfully distinct from a Jobs review.

### Versus Fusheng

The review does not open with a sharp contrarian thesis or search for a single
`破局点`. It moves through concern, evidence, trust, and 90-day execution. This
is meaningfully distinct from a Fusheng review.

### Versus Zhang Yiming

The review does not mainly analyze information flow, data systems, or
organizational abstraction. It stays closer to user regret, physical-product
trust, service, and brand promise after purchase. This is meaningfully distinct
from a Zhang Yiming review.
