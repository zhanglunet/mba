## Verdict

QiAnXin is a competent government security contractor that has captured a regulatory moat. That's a defensible business, but it's not an influential brand. The AI security pivot is the most interesting thing they're doing, and if they execute it well, it could change my view significantly. But right now, I see a company optimized for compliance and government relationships — not for first-principles technical disruption. The world needs better security. QiAnXin could be the company that provides it for China. But "the best government security vendor in China" is a much smaller prize than "the company that reinvented how security works."

---

## Critical Gap

The question I'd ask QiAnXin: **What would you build if there were no government procurement contracts?** If the honest answer is "we don't know" or "nothing," then they have a mission problem, not a brand problem. The best companies — Tesla, SpaceX, xAI — exist because they have something they desperately want to exist in the world, independent of whether the government is paying for it. **QiAnXin needs a product that users want, not just a product that procurement officers approve.** AISOC is a step in the right direction — can it be deployed by a 100-person company without a full government IT procurement cycle? Can it run on-premise at a school, a hospital, a startup? If the answer is yes, that's where the real brand story lives. If the answer is "that's not our customer," then the ceiling is what it is.

## Brand Action（90 天）

1. **Publish the QiAnXin AI Security Benchmark**: Define what "good" looks like for AI-driven security operations — publish the metrics, open source the evaluation framework. Make the benchmark something the global security community adopts. Own the standard, own the brand.
2. **Launch a free tier for SMB / open source security tools**: Give small businesses access to QiAnXin's threat intelligence via API. Build a developer community around security data. Create an ecosystem that doesn't require a government procurement cycle.
3. **Hire a world-class western security researcher as Chief Security Scientist**: Someone like a Mikko Hyppönen or a Katie Moussouris — a person with global credibility who can represent QiAnXin's technical capabilities on the international stage. One credible external face changes the global perception immediately.
