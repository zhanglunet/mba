**Verdict:** The company has pieces of a real security platform, but the brand is trying to hide the hard question: what exact threat does it stop better than anyone else?

**Quote:** “If you can’t tell me the primitive threat model, you don’t have a security strategy. You have a product catalog.”

**Critical gap:** The report lacks hard performance claims: detection latency, response time, false positives, deployment scale, cost reduction.

**Brand action:** Publish one brutal benchmark around a specific threat class and build the entire brand around beating that constraint.
