Long-term games attract long-term people: the researchers, the enterprise customers who want a stable partner, the governments seeking a trustworthy counterparty. This creates a brand that compounds in relationship capital, not just revenue.

## Dissent Memo

The one thing I'd push on: the safety narrative, as currently told, is abstract. What does it feel like to the user when they interact with a "safe" AI? If users can't articulate the specific benefit of Anthropic's safety approach in their own experience, it's not genuine specific knowledge — it's marketing. Claude Code is the exception: developers feel the quality difference. The challenge is translating that felt quality into brand narrative across all of Anthropic's products, not just code generation.
