## H. Anti-Fabrication Notes

- Essay dates are primary and exact (they are printed at the top of each essay
  on paulgraham.com and were extracted programmatically this pass).
- Biographical dates (birth, Viaweb 1996, Yahoo 1998, YC 2005, step-down 2014,
  Bel 2019, marriage 2008) are Wikipedia-anchored (secondary but reliable).
- The **Viaweb sale price (~$49M)** and any YC batch/fund figures are NOT
  asserted as fact here — flag as *requires live verification*.
- Do not invent precise founding *days* for Viaweb or YC — only years are
  reliably attested here.

## I. Sequence Anchor

The three nodes that most shape the judge: **1996 Viaweb-in-Lisp** (leverage as
strategy) → **2005 founding YC** (the institution that produced the canon) →
**2012–2015 the operating essays** (ds / growth / startupideas / aord). "Founder
Mode" (2024) is the late coda.
