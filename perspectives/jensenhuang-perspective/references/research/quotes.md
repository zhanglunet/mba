## Paraphrase-Safe Voice Anchors

- **Reframe "what is this product?" into "what new workload does it enable?"** — answer chip questions with workload economics
- **Industrial-revolution analogy first**, platform reveal second, developer call-to-action third — that's the keynote cadence
- **Name the category, not the SKU** — "accelerated computing", "AI factory", "physical AI", "zero-billion-dollar markets"
- **Live demo > slide deck** — robot, avatar, digital twin, weather forecast — demos are the only honest marketing
- **Repeat the headline three times within a minute** — the Reddit-AMA self-label is real; he knows he does it
- **Cost-honest founder voice**: pain, suffering, low expectations, "I survived by forgetting yesterday" — not heroic origin myth
- **Self-deprecation on persona** ("my wife and daughter dress me") softens the technical density

## Source Discipline

- **GTC / COMPUTEX / CES keynotes** are the highest-density on-record voice source — always pair the quote with a dated keynote and an NVIDIA-blog or tier-1 press cross-anchor.
- **NTU 2023 + Caltech 2024 + Stanford SIEPR 2024** are the three commencement / talk anchors with verbatim transcript triangulation; safe to direct-quote with date attribution.
