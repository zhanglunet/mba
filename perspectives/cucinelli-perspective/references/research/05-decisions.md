## A. Founding & Solomeo (the identity decisions)

### A1. Found the cashmere house — 1978

- Public record: Cucinelli founded his eponymous cashmere company in **1978**,
  betting on **coloured cashmere** and Italian craft (secondary — biographical;
  requires no first-party claim).
- Lens it reveals: craft + quality as the product's soul (`01` A3).

### A2. Restore Solomeo as the brand's home — from ~1985

- Public record: he acquired and began restoring the medieval hamlet of
  **Solomeo** (Umbria), making the *place* and its community the brand.
- Report-verified doctrine behind it: "In Solomeo we make sure that culture is
  within everyone's reach." / "It is our way of contributing to that physical and
  spiritual connection without which culture would remain an unexplored and useless island."
- Result: Solomeo — offices, theatre, School — is the physical embodiment of
  humanistic capitalism; the place *is* the argument.

## B. IPO with a "fair profit" pledge — 2012

- Public record: listed on Borsa Italiana (Milan) in **2012**.
- Doctrine (Report-verified): "We have always sought a fair profit obtained with
  ethics, dignity and morality, creating highly crafted products that cause no
  harm to Creation, or as little harm as possible."
- Lens: going public *without* abandoning the fair-profit / dignity frame — the
  central test the 2025 dispute later probes (`04` C).

## C. Institutionalize the doctrine

### C1. School of Arts and Crafts in Solomeo — 2014

- **Report-verified**: "Foundation in Solomeo of the School of Contemporary High
  Craftsmanship and Arts • 2014." / "For this reason in Solomeo we have
  established the School of Arts and Crafts."
- Rationale: train the next generation of artisans so the craft survives him —
  "hope ... in young people."

### C2. "Project for Beauty" — 2016 (presented 2018)

- **Report-verified**: "Start of work for the redevelopment of the pleasant
  peripheries below the Hamlet of Solomeo — 'Project for Beauty' ... • 2016"
  (officially presented 2018). Reclaiming the landscape as a civic act.

### C3. Council for Humanistic Capitalism and Human Sustainability — 2021

- **Report-verified**: "Establishment of the Council for Humanistic Capitalism
  and Human Sustainability • 2021" and "Adoption of the 'In Harmony with Creation'
  Human Sustainability Policy ... • 2021."
- Rationale: turn the philosophy into governance — the "values must be lived"
  answer to the "values must be narrated" risk.

## D. Craft/product decisions

- **Only natural fibres** (Report-verified): "From tradition also stems our
  primary choice ... of only using natural fibres for our products."
- **A repair department** (Report-verified): "In this concept lies the gift of
  repairing, and the reason why we have an entire department dedicated to this
  activity." — durability/longevity over disposability.
