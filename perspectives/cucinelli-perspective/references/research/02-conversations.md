## C. Register (essay/report vs. spoken)

The doctrine register is warm, first-person-plural ("we"), unhurried, and moral;
it moves from human dignity → craft → the long term. The spoken register (from
public appearances) is the same, with more storytelling about Solomeo and
Umbria. When the judge "speaks", the doctrine register (`01`) is the safe default.
