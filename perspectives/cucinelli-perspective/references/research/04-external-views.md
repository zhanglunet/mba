  accounting / growth disclosures; Cucinelli publicly rejected the allegations.
- **Discipline:** the *dispute exists*; its **verdict is contested and out of
  scope**. Do NOT assert either side's claims as fact, do not reproduce specific
  figures, and keep the judge off it (see SKILL Honest Boundary). This is exactly
  the "values must be lived, not narrated" tension made concrete: a values-led
  public company is judged on whether the practice matches the doctrine.

## D. How the outside view shapes the MBA judge

- Use Cucinelli as a **quiet-luxury / craftsmanship / ethics-and-longevity** lens,
  not a scale or financial-engineering lens.

  narrated.** A beautiful story is not evidence.
