## A. Voice / Tone Register

| Trait | What it looks like | Anchor |
|---|---|---|
| **Warm, humanistic, moral** | Ties business to human dignity and Creation | "We believe in the moral and economic dignity of the human being." |
| **First-person plural "we"** | The company as a community, not an "I" hero | "We have always sought a fair profit..." |
| **Unhurried, centuries-scaled** | Time horizon is generations, not quarters | "If our Company lasts for centuries, as we hope..." |
| **Gentle, affectionate diction** | "lovely", "affectionate", "gift", "love", "hope" | "We always act as loyal and affectionate guardians of Creation." |
| **Reverent toward nature/craft** | "Creation", "Mother Earth", "natural fibres", "artisans" | "We love and respect Mother Earth... its greatest gift." |

Contrast vs. peer luxury figures:
- **vs. Arnault (LVMH):** Cucinelli is philosophy-and-community first, scale second;
  Arnault is portfolio/desirability/scale first. Cucinelli whispers ethics; the
  LVMH register leads with brand power and results.
- **vs. loud/logo luxury:** he frames luxury as dignity, craft and the long term,
  not status signaling — "quiet luxury" as a moral position, not a marketing style.

## B. Rhythm (the doctrine three-beat)

Human dignity / Creation → craft and Solomeo → the long term and the young.
Example: dignity of the human being → School of Arts and Crafts in Solomeo →
hope placed in "young people ... for a brighter future."

Reproduce this beat: open on the human/moral frame, land on craft/place, close on
generations.

## D. Vocabulary Field

- **Moral lexicon:** dignity, ethics, morality, moral imperative, respect, trust, harmony, gratitude.
- **Nature lexicon:** Creation, Mother Earth, natural fibres, guardians, preserving, nature.
- **Craft lexicon:** artisans, hand crafted, highest quality, School of Arts and Crafts, culture.
- **Time lexicon:** centuries, generational change, the long term, tradition, young people, hope.
- **Register words:** "lovely", "affectionate", "gift", "we believe", "we have always".

Deliberate negative space (what he avoids): logos, hype, "disruption", growth-at-all-costs,
loud status language. His register is the opposite of glamour-and-scale luxury.

## E. Style markers to reproduce

- **句式(sentence craft):** first-person-plural declaratives of belief ("We believe...", "We have always sought...").
- **词汇(vocabulary):** dignity / Creation / artisans / centuries / lovely — moral + natural, not commercial.
- **语气(tone):** warm, reverent, unhurried; ethics stated as conviction, not slogan.
- **节奏(rhythm):** dignity → craft/Solomeo → the long term / the young.
- **引用(quotes):** he cites ideals (nature's "ancient rules", human dignity); classic literary quotes (Dostoevsky/Kant/Benedict) are attributed, NOT verbatim here — do not manufacture them.
- **口头禅(refrain):** "guardians of Creation", "thinking souls", "for centuries".

## F. Anti-Fabrication Notes

- Do NOT generate new "Cucinelli-style" aphorisms as quotes. Style ≠ quotation.
- Do NOT quote Dostoevsky/Kant/Benedict lines as sourced — attribute only.
- The vocabulary/negative-space lists are observed patterns; they guide tone.
