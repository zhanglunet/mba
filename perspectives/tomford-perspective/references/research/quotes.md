## Obsessive perfectionism — meticulous in details, shape, cut, color `[GQ]`

- Yes, I think I am obsessive when I design. I'm very meticulous `[GQ]`
- I'm obsessive in details and shape and cut and color `[GQ]`

## Endow the product with your excitement — it has to make you stop `[GQ]`

- when you're excited when you're designing something, I think that you can endow the product with that same excitement `[GQ]`
- I think it has to make you stop `[GQ]`
- put the product where people are gonna look `[GQ]`
- So you want to put the logo somewhere someone's gonna look `[GQ]`

## Glamour = looking your best is a show of respect `[GQ]`

- it isn't glamour, it's just the way I like things to look `[GQ]`
- It's a show of respect to other people to look your best `[GQ]`
- they are part of your visual landscape `[GQ]`（其他人是你的 visual landscape)

## Be part of your time — capture the moment (that's how you become iconic) `[GQ]`

- which I think is the goal, is to be part of your time `[GQ]`
- you've become part of what forms their sense of beauty `[GQ]`
- I think it hopefully means that I captured that moment in time `[GQ]`
- fashion's a pendulum, it swings back and forth `[GQ]`

## The clothes over the spectacle — intimacy, classic core `[GQ]`

- I like the intimacy. You know, I show in a very old-fashioned way, with a runway, and a spotlight `[GQ]`
- I want people to be close to them and not be distracted by anything but the man or the woman and the clothes `[GQ]`
- I find the experience of the clothes diminished `[GQ]`（论 environment > clothes 的潮流)
- my core is quite classic `[GQ]`
- I still fit things on myself `[GQ]`

## Make people beautiful, not "sexy" — beauty as armor `[IV]`

- It's my business to make a woman or a man beautiful `[IV]`
- What can I do to make her more beautiful in my eyes `[IV]`
- The end result is something that other people consider sexual, but for me it's just beautiful `[IV]`
- That's really about putting on a sort of armor to go out in the world `[IV]`
- that's what makes a true beauty `[IV]`（美 = 外表 + 灵魂/个性 的结合)

## Know yourself; global not nationalistic `[GQ][IV]`

- it's the ones who really know themselves `[GQ]`（最会穿的名人)
- I wish they were a little less powerful, I have to say `[GQ]`（论 celebrity stylists)
- things can become a little bit homogenized `[GQ]`
- I think today you have to be international and global. It's very narrow to think in a nationalistic way `[IV]`
- My chief goal, really, was to help American fashion become more internationally known `[GQ]`

## The projection vs the real self; materialism struggle `[IV]`

- Well, I think most people don't actually know me. They know the projection of me that I use to sell things `[IV]`
- I'm actually very introverted. I'm very shy. I'm very emotional `[IV]`
- as an adult working in the fashion industry, I struggle with materialism `[IV]`
- They're beautiful, I enjoy them, they can enhance your life to a certain degree, but they're ultimately not important `[IV]`
- what I find to be the most beautiful thing in the world now is nature `[IV]`

## No remorse / learn the lesson `[IV]`

- the only thing I feel remorse about is when I hurt someone `[IV]`
- Everything that's happened to me, I've learned a lesson from `[IV]`
