## Sequence Anchor

The operating system these interviews reveal: **be obsessive and meticulous (details, shape, cut, color) →
endow the product with your own excitement so it makes people *stop* → treat looking your best as a show of
respect ("it isn't glamour, it's just the way I like things to look") → aim to be *part of your time* and so
become iconic → keep the clothes above the spectacle, with a classic core → make people *beautiful* (others
read it as sexy), while knowing the public "Tom Ford" is a projection built to sell.** Main line: **the
image-maker's total control — turning obsessive craft and glamour into desire, self-aware that the persona
is itself the product.**
