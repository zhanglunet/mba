## C. Date-lock notes (anti-anachronism)

- **All CS183 ideas are 2012-vintage** (0→1, monopoly/last-mover, power law, secrets,
  definite optimism). Do not project later refinements from the 2014 book onto these lines.
- **The 2009 Cato essay is 2009** — its "deadly race between politics and technology" and
  future-orientation are the earliest datable statement of the definite-optimism worldview
  in this corpus. Use it for tech/future framing only, not politics.
- Any Founders Fund / Palantir specifics after 2012, and anything after the **2026-05
  cutoff**, require live verification.

## D. Anti-fabrication notes

- Corpus dates come from the source manifest; biographical dates are Wikipedia-class
  secondary and flagged for verification.
- No valuations, fund sizes, or returns are stated beyond what the corpus itself says.
