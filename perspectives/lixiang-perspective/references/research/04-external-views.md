## View 1 — MEGA Exposed Rhythm And Category-Market Misread

External coverage of MEGA consistently framed the issue as more than one model's
weak launch. It revealed a mismatch between pure-BEV infrastructure, user
perception, sales expectation and organizational focus.

Source anchors:

  https://www.21jingji.com/article/20240321/herald/5e4b1e073b6fbcd3878dc99ded0970f6.html

  https://www.pingwest.com/a/293455
- The Paper, self-criticism recap:
  https://www.thepaper.cn/newsDetail_forward_26760326

MBA implication:

- **Real-world signal:** launch attention is weak if the usage scene is not
  ready.
- **Leverage quality:** charging network and sales focus are leverage, not
  afterthoughts.

## View 2 — "Family SUV" Is Powerful But Can Become A Cage

Li Auto's family-user positioning is clear and commercially effective, but
external observers often ask whether it limits brand imagination beyond large
range-extended SUVs.

Source anchors:

- Li Auto 2024 annual report / product lineup:
  https://www.sec.gov/Archives/edgar/data/1791706/000141057825000678/li-20241231x20f.htm
- Q2 2024 earnings call:
  https://www.fool.com/earnings/call-transcripts/2024/08/28/li-auto-li-q2-2024-earnings-call-transcript/

MBA implication:

- **Category coinage:** strong scene language can become a moat.
- **Identity coherence:** the moat becomes a prison if new products cannot be
  explained through the same scene logic.

## View 3 — Strong Margins And Service Network Are Real, But Not Permanent

Investor-facing documents show service coverage, smart-space claims and margins.
The outside risk is that price competition and new BEV bets pressure the clean
family-SUV formula.

MBA implication:

- A Li Xiang judge should not reward "family" as a slogan. It must connect to
  margin, service, charging, safety and repeat use.

## External Guardrails

1. Do not turn Li Auto's MEGA mistake into permanent brand failure.
2. Do not let one successful scene justify every future model.
3. Always ask: what exact family / user scene is this product solving?
