## A. Build (Microsoft developer conference, annual May)

### A1. Build 2023 keynote — "AI platform shift" debut (2023-05-23)

- Microsoft PDF transcript: https://news.microsoft.com/wp-content/uploads/prod/2023/05/KEY01-Build-Satya-Nadella-Keynote.pdf
- Recap: https://www.techradar.com/pro/major-platform-shifts-are-in-the-air-microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-outlines-how-copilot-is-going-to-change-everything-about-how-you-work
- Opening frame: timeline of computing from Vannevar Bush's "As We May Think" essay → ChatGPT — Nadella anchors current moment in a long arc of "tools for thought."
- Key formulation, the **Copilot Stack**: "AI infrastructure → foundation models → AI orchestration → Copilot → Copilot extensibility."
- Calls Microsoft Fabric "perhaps the biggest launch of a data product from Microsoft since the launch of SQL Server."
- Voice tempo: customer mission → platform shift → developer extensibility → governance/safety closer.

### A2. Build 2024 keynote (2024-05-21)

- Microsoft PDF transcript: https://news.microsoft.com/wp-content/uploads/prod/2024/05/Satya-Nadella_Transcript_KEY01_Build2024.pdf
- Theme: from chat to agents; Copilot Studio for custom agents; GPT-4o on Azure same-day as OpenAI launch; Cohere, Databricks, Meta, Mistral, Snowflake models on Azure AI.
- Pinned claim that recurs in later keynotes: "GitHub Copilot saves 50% of coding time for developers."
- Companion announcement same week: Mustafa Suleyman now leading Microsoft AI (see `05-decisions.md`).

### A3. Build 2025 keynote — "AI agents" pivot (2025-05)

- Recap: https://www.reworked.co/digital-workplace/microsoft-build-2025-microsoft-pins-its-future-on-ai-agents/
- Announces Azure AI Foundry Agent Service GA; multi-agent workflows as first-class building blocks.
- The phrase "the open agentic web" recurs in this keynote and in later interviews.

## B. Ignite (Microsoft IT pro / customer conference, annual Nov)

### B1. Ignite 2018 — "Tech intensity" coined

- Recap: https://www.computerweekly.com/microscope/news/252449196/Nadella-calls-for-more-tech-intensity
- Original definition: companies must both adopt best-in-class tech AND build their own digital capabilities; (tech adoption) ^ (tech capabilities) = tech intensity.
- This is the keynote that gives Microsoft its 2018–2021 strategy vocabulary.

### B2. Ignite 2023 — "We are the Copilot company" (2023-11-15)

- Recap: https://redmondmag.com/articles/2023/11/16/nadella-ignite-2023-keynote.aspx
- Verge / TechRadar recap: https://www.technologyrecord.com/article/microsoft-ignite-satya-nadella-discusses-new-copilot-agents-the-future-of-windows-and-more-in-keynote-speech
- Direct line (cross-quoted across recaps): "We are the Copilot company. We believe in a future where there will be a Copilot for everyone and everything you do."
- Companion line: "Microsoft Copilot is that one experience that runs across all our surfaces, understanding your context on the Web, on your device. And when you're at work, bringing the right skills to you when you need them."
- Hardware reveal: Maia 100 custom AI accelerator; Cobalt 100 ARM CPU.
- Sets up Copilot as Microsoft's "new UI for AI."

### B3. Ignite 2024 keynote (2024-11-19)

- Microsoft PDF transcript: https://pub-c2c1d9230f0b4abb9b0d2d95e06fd4ef.r2.dev/2024/11/11192024-Ignite-KEY01-Satya-Nadella2.pdf
- Recap: https://www.uctoday.com/unified-communications/microsoft-ignite-2024-five-key-takeaways-from-satya-nadellas-keynote/
- Frame: "Copilot is the UI for AI; agents are the apps."
- Returns to security: SFI as company-wide pre-condition before any feature ships.

## C. Inspire (Microsoft partner conference)

### C1. Inspire 2017 — "We have an unprecedented opportunity"

- Microsoft Stories page: https://news.microsoft.com/features/nadella-to-partners-we-have-an-unprecedented-opportunity/
- Nadella to partners: Microsoft has "always been a partner-led company"; ecosystem drives local economies.
- This keynote codifies the **platforms-and-partners** doctrine that runs through every subsequent Inspire / partner event.

- Recap: https://www.technologyrecord.com/article/microsoft-inspire-satya-nadella-reveals-new-microsoft-ai-cloud-partner-program-in-keynote-speech
- Reframes the partner program around AI Cloud — Azure + Microsoft 365 Copilot + Dynamics 365 + Security Copilot.

## D. Davos / World Economic Forum

### D1. Davos 2024 conversation (2024-01-16)

- WEF podcast: https://www.weforum.org/podcasts/meet-the-leader/episodes/davos-2024-conversation-microsoft-satya-nadella/
- WEF essay: https://www.weforum.org/stories/2024/01/microsoft-ceo-ai-technology-consequences/
- CNN recap: https://www.cnn.com/2024/01/16/tech/microsoft-ceo-satya-nadella-talks-ai-at-davos/index.html
- Key line (across recaps): "We have to take the unintended consequences of any new technology along with all the benefits, and think about them simultaneously."
- Endorses globally-aligned AI norms and standards.

### D2. Davos 2026 — Energy + sovereignty (2026-01)

- Conversation with BlackRock CEO Larry Fink.
- CNBC: https://www.cnbc.com/2026/01/20/microsoft-nadella-ai-race-energy-tokens.html
- The Register: https://www.theregister.com/2026/01/21/nadella_ai_sovereignty_wef/
- Core line (CNBC recap): "We will quickly lose even the social permission to actually take something like energy, which is a scarce resource, and use it to generate these tokens, if these tokens are not improving health outcomes, education outcomes, public sector efficiency, private sector competitiveness across all sectors."
- Reframes AI sovereignty: not where your servers are, but who controls models trained on your proprietary knowledge.
