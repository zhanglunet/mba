## Provenance: how to read these

- Both talks are **his monologues** — all quotes are his own words. Where he **quotes others**
  (teachers' emails "flip the classroom", students' feedback, Khanmigo's own responses), it is labeled.
- The **2023 talk is a live product demo of Khanmigo** (Khan Academy's own AI). His "This isn't a fake
  demo" is itself a tell that these are **self-interested product claims** — treat as marketing, not
  independent evidence (see SKILL Self-Conflict & Honest Boundary).
- His books (*The One World Schoolhouse*, *Brave New Words*) are **not** in this corpus and are not quoted.

## Anti-Fabrication notes
