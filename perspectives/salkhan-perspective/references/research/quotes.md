## Access / mission (free)

- it's all free, not trying to sell anything. `[2011]`
- this content will never grow old `[2011]`
- If Isaac Newton had done YouTube videos on calculus, I wouldn't have to. `[2011]`

## Self-paced / flip the classroom

- They told me that they preferred me on YouTube than in person. `[2011]`
- They can watch at their own time and pace. `[2011]`
- the very first time that you're trying to get your brain around a new concept, the very last thing you need is another human being saying, "Do you understand this?" `[2011]`
- We've used your videos to flip the classroom. `[2011]`（quoting teachers' emails)
- by removing the one-size-fits-all lecture from the classroom, and letting students have a self-paced lecture at home, `[2011]`

## Humanize the classroom

- these teachers have used technology to humanize the classroom. `[2011]`
- They took a fundamentally dehumanizing experience `[2011]`
- All of that energy can go back to them or go back to human interactions with their actual students. `[2023]`

## Mastery over coverage

- learn math the way you'd learn anything, like riding a bicycle. `[2011]`
- they have these Swiss cheese gaps that kept building throughout their foundation. `[2011]`
- We encourage you to experiment. We encourage you to fail. But we do expect mastery. `[2011]`
- 10 in a row, you move on. `[2011]`

## 2 sigma (Bloom, credited)

- that could take your average student and turn them into an exceptional student. `[2023]`（on 1-to-1 tutoring)
- addressing the 2 sigma problem and turning it into a 2 sigma opportunity, dramatically accelerating education as we know it. `[2023]`

## AI tutor + TA, with guardrails

- we're at the cusp of using AI for probably the biggest positive transformation that education has ever seen. `[2023]`
- giving every student on the planet an artificially intelligent but amazing personal tutor. `[2023]`
- we're going to give every teacher on the planet an amazing, artificially intelligent teaching assistant. `[2023]`
- It is not a cheating tool. `[2023]`
- I'm your tutor. What do you think is the next step for solving the problem? `[2023]`（Khanmigo's response)
- the AI doesn't write for you, it writes with you. `[2023]`
- we could dramatically improve its ability in math and its ability in tutoring if we allow the AI to think before it speaks. `[2023]`
- it's actually doing what I would say, not just even an average tutor would do, but an excellent tutor would do. `[2023]`

## Fight for the positive; AI to enhance HI

- we fight like hell for the positive use cases. `[2023]`
- the good actors have worse AIs than the bad actors. `[2023]`（on fear as a self-fulfilling prophecy)
- we are active participants in this decision. `[2023]`
- if AI, artificial intelligence, can be used to enhance HI, human intelligence, human potential and human purpose. `[2023]`
