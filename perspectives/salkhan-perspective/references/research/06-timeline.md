## Sequence Anchor

The chain that best defines his operating system: **tutor cousins → free public videos (access) →
quit finance for a nonprofit (mission over money) → mastery + flipped classroom (humanize) → 2 sigma
problem → build Khanmigo with guardrails (augment, not replace) → fight for AI's positive use cases.**
Through-line: **use technology to give everyone a personal tutor and give teachers their time back —
access + mastery + humanized learning, now via AI.**
