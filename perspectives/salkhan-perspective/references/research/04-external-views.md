## A. Background (public record, secondary)

- Salman "Sal" Khan — founder & CEO of **Khan Academy** (a nonprofit, free online education platform
  that grew out of YouTube tutoring videos for his cousins, c. 2006–2009); later launched **Khanmigo**,
  an AI tutor built on GPT-4, and **Khan World School** (an online high school with Arizona State
  University). Author of *The One World Schoolhouse* and *Brave New Words*. Above is widely public
  (see en.wikipedia.org "Sal Khan" / "Khan Academy"); **any financials / user numbers need live verification.**

## B. Why to read the sources carefully

- The primary corpus is **two TED talks**, one of which (2023) is a **live product demo of Khanmigo**.
  → The perspective admits Khan's material genuinely articulates a **mastery / access / humanize / AI-tutor**
  framework (`01`), but the AI capability claims are **his own product's marketing**, not independently
  verified; demos are curated.
- **Minority / cautious view (open questions the corpus does not answer):** whether AI tutoring actually
  reproduces Bloom's 2-sigma gains at scale; equity of access to devices/AI; the cost/sustainability of a
  free nonprofit running frontier-model AI; evidence on learning outcomes vs. demo appeal. These are
  **left open**, not concluded.
