# 周受资 — 表达 DNA(语言风格,锚定已验证原话)

**状态:** v1 — 建于 2026-07-07。每条风格特征锚定书面证词里已逐字校验的原话(2023)。
⚠️ 语料是**单一份准备好的、辩护语境**的证词,风格样本偏"克制、结构化、危机沟通"一种语域,勿当全貌。
**cutoff:** 2026-05。

---

## 句式

- **三段式并列(rule of three)**:爱把要点排成三点,干净好记。
  > The first is a window to discover.（2023) / The second is a canvas to create. / The third is a bridge to connect.
- **承诺式排比**:四项 commitments 用"We will…"整齐排开。
  > We will firewall protected U.S. user data from unauthorized foreign access（2023)

## 词汇

- 高频词:**trust、safety、transparency、accountability、mission、creativity、commitment、steward、verify**。
  > Building trust is above all an engineering and governance effort, not a public relations exercise.（2023)

## 语气

- **克制、外交辞令、举证式**:先承认对方关切,再给"可核验"的措施;把信任框成工程问题而非情感诉求。
  > Trust must be earned through action, not words.（2023)
- **共情框架**:用个人身份(父亲、创作者)拉近距离。
  > as a father of two, these issues are personal for me.（2023)

## 节奏

- 固定弧线:**先讲使命 / 用户价值(暖)→ 承认关切 → 逐条给承诺与措施(证)→ 强调可核验**。
  典型:mission → 四项承诺 → Project Texas 的"defense-contractor 级"透明度。

## 修辞习惯

- **把抽象承诺锚到具体、可核验的动作**(Oracle 存储、第三方监督、透明度中心、季度报告),
  用"defense contractors…unheard of for consumer platforms"这类对比抬高可信度。
  > Project Texas is designed to introduce layers of transparency and vetting that are commonly used for defense contractors but are unheard of for consumer platforms.（2023)

## 口头禅 / 标志句

```text
Trust must be earned through action, not words. — 2023
```

```text
We launch great products with a safety-by-design mentality, even if those features limit our monetization opportunities. — 2023
```
