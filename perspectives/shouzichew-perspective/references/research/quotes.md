## 使命 / 产品哲学

- our mission to inspire creativity and bring joy `[2023]`
- The first is a window to discover. `[2023]`
- The second is a canvas to create. `[2023]`
- The third is a bridge to connect. `[2023]`
- Although some people may still think of TikTok as a dancing app for teenagers, the reality is that our platform and our community have become so much more for so many. `[2023]`
- While users in the United States represent 10 percent of our global community, their voice accounts for 25 percent of the total views around the world. `[2023]`

## 创作者 / 小企业经济

- we pride ourselves on being a platform that helps companies—many of them small businesses—thrive. `[2023]`
- you'll hear incredibly powerful stories of discovery, creation, and connection that contribute to people's livelihoods and well-being. `[2023]`

## 信任靠行动赢得(工程与治理,不是公关)

- I know that trust is something that is earned through action, not words `[2023]`
- Trust must be earned through action, not words. `[2023]`
- Building trust is above all an engineering and governance effort, not a public relations exercise. `[2023]`
- We will go above and beyond, so our community and regulators can see and verify our actions. `[2023]`
- Congress and the American people understandably want proof that we are living up to our ideals. `[2023]`

## 安全优先于短期变现

- We launch great products with a safety-by-design mentality, even if those features limit our monetization opportunities. `[2023]`
- we have made decisions that prioritize safety over short-term commercial success. `[2023]`
- we do not allow paid political ads on the platform, even though they could be a source of significant revenue. `[2023]`
- Safety and wellness—in particular for teens—is a core priority for TikTok. `[2023]`
- Trust and safety represents our largest labor expense for TikTok's U.S. operations. `[2023]`

## 承认错误 / 责任托管

- Another important part of being a responsible steward of user data is owning up to our mistakes and making changes to address them. `[2023]`
- I condemn this misconduct in the strongest possible terms. `[2023]`

## 四项承诺 / 独立性（**stated commitments,非独立核实**)

- We will keep safety—particularly for teenagers—a top priority for us `[2023]`
- We will firewall protected U.S. user data from unauthorized foreign access `[2023]`
- Tiktok will remain a platform for free expression and will not be manipulated by any government `[2023]`
- We will be transparent and give access to third-party independent monitors, to remain accountable for our commitments. `[2023]`
- ByteDance is not an agent of China or any other country. `[2023]`
- there is no way for the Chinese government to access it or compel access to it. `[2023]`

## Project Texas / 本地化

- The centerpiece of our work is called Project Texas. `[2023]`
- Project Texas is designed to introduce layers of transparency and vetting that are commonly used for defense contractors but are unheard of for consumer platforms. `[2023]`
- To ensure that the data of all Americans is stored in America and hosted by an American headquartered company, we have contracted with Oracle `[2023]`

## 背景 / 自述

- A third-generation Singaporean `[2023]`
- Long before I became CEO, I was a content creator `[2023]`
- as a father of two, these issues are personal for me. `[2023]`
- As CEO, I consider the safety of the platform to be paramount. `[2023]`
