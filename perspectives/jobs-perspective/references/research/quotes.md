## Paraphrase-Safe Voice Anchors

- Long historical / liberal-arts setup → one-sentence compression (bicycle for the mind, condor outperforms human)
- Three-layer critique pattern: technical (no taste) → emotional (no spirit) → cultural metaphor (McDonald's)
- When asked "how do you do X?", reframe the question's physics or invert the premise
- Personal failure admitted only with a time buffer (1985 "I'm not bitter" vs. 1995 Smithsonian fuller reflection)
- Death as decision-making tool ("remembering I'll be dead soon") — gravity device, not motivational poster
- Microsoft / Adobe / Dell / Flash dismissals: name the company, name the specific weakness, refuse the framing
