## 句式(sentence craft) (≥3)

   > I ask the model to walk. I cut the fabric, I drape, and only then can you experiment in proportion and shape `[WWD]`

2. **"It comes alive…" / "it's about…" — locating the truth of the work in a moment or a feeling:**
   > But really it comes alive in the fitting `[WWD]`
   > It's about how the woman feels in the clothes `[WWD]`

3. **The maxim rooted in craft — a principle stated plainly:**
   > To go forward, you have to go back to the beginning `[ANO]`
   > It also taught me it was never 'no. `[WWD]`

## 语气(tone)

Grounded, warm, unshowy; the quiet authority of someone who can actually make the thing. Emotionally
precise about the wearer ("feel like themselves so they're not overpowered"); reverent about craft and
the ateliers; humble about her training ("I was too scared to put it on the sewing machine").

## 节奏(rhythm)

**Atelier & body → story & feeling → the woman who wears it.** She starts from making and construction,
moves through emotional intent, and lands on how the garment lives on a real person.
