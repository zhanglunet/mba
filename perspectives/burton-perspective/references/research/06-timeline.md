## Sequence Anchor

The operating system these interviews reveal: **learn the craft hands-on in the atelier (McQueen, Hoxton
Square basement) → make beauty out of nothing, "it was never 'no'" → design look-by-look with a couture
mindset, judging it in the fitting on a real body → land on how the woman *feels* (armor, but still
herself) → and when you inherit a founder's house, go back to its DNA yet speak to the woman of today.**
Main line: **the maker's hands — craft and the fitting as the truth of luxury, stewarding a legacy while
authoring for the present.**
