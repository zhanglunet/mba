## Sequence Anchor

The operating system these letters reveal: **make each brand *ever more desirable* → run it as a
family-controlled group with a *long-term vision*, looking beyond the figures → treat each Maison as
*eternal*, drawing on legacy to build its place in history → power it with *quality × creativity* through
*decentralized, entrepreneurial* Maisons and star designers → *honor the past AND invent the future* → and
convert margins into *exceptional investment capacity*, cash flow as opportunity.** Main line: **the
consolidator-builder — compounding desirability across a portfolio of timeless Maisons, patient capital and
star creativity, for the long term.**
