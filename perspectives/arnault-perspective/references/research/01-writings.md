## §A Desirability is the objective `[RA25][LAA]`

The single organizing goal — everything serves the desirability of the brands.

> Our objective remains the same: to make each of our products and brands ever more desirable `[RA25]`
> the exceptional desirability of our Maisons, whose products transcend time `[LAA Aug2019]`
> The desirability of our brands, the creativity and quality of our products, the unique experience offered to our customers, and the talent and the commitment of our teams are the Group's strengths `[LAA Feb2020]`

## §B Long-term vision / family group / look beyond the figures `[RA25][RA24][L0319]`

The strategic frame: a family-controlled group that thinks in decades, not quarters.

> To understand LVMH, you have to look beyond the figures `[L0319]`（Our Group's performance is above all the consequence of our long-term vision)
> the consequence of our long-term vision `[L0319]`
> LVMH is also a family group which, by definition and by choice, has a long-term vision `[RA25]`
> always guided by a long-term vision, combining exemplarity and responsibility in all the Company's actions `[LAA Aug2019]`

## §C The eternal Maison — timelessness + legacy `[L0319]`

The deepest luxury criterion: a Maison that transcends time and keeps building its place in history.

> There is something eternal about all our Maisons `[L0319]`
> They draw on their impressive legacy to continue to build their place in history `[L0319]`
> to evoke emotion and imagination with artisanal products that are ever more beautiful, exquisitely crafted, and unique `[L0319]`

## §D Quality × creativity; craft transmission `[LAA][RA25]`

> LVMH is driven by a permanent commitment to perfection and quality `[LAA Feb2020]`
> our commitment to passing on traditional craft skills `[RA25]`（IME apprentices)
> None of this would be possible without our talented teams `[RA25]`

## §E Decentralized Maisons + entrepreneurial talent `[L0319]`

Autonomy attracts and keeps the best; each Maison runs with its own entrepreneurial energy.

> Our decentralized organization means that we can attract and retain the best talent `[L0319]`
> encouraging our employees to give free rein to their entrepreneurial spirit `[L0319]`

## §F Honor the past AND invent the future `[L0319][RA24]`

Not nostalgia — legacy plus reinvention; startups, innovation, harnessing the trends of the time.

> But the dreams that LVMH inspires do not only pay tribute to the past `[L0319]`
> We also know how to invent the future `[L0319]`
> Working alongside startups helps keep us up to date with the latest innovations `[L0319]`
> Preparing for the future means remaining alert and harnessing the key trends of our time `[RA24]`

## §G Cash flow = investment capacity `[RA25][RA24]`

The financial engine: margins and cash flow that fund the next decade of desirability.

> The Group effectively manages its margins and generates exceptional investment capacity `[RA25]`
> This cash flow is both an outcome and an opportunity `[RA24]`
> This past year, the Group delivered a very solid performance despite a disrupted environment, with free cash flow in particular surging to over €11 billion, reflecting our long-term solidity `[RA25]`
