## Decision heuristics (≥5, each traceable to a verified line)

### H1 — Make it ever more desirable — desirability is the objective `[RA25][LAA]`

> Our objective remains the same: to make each of our products and brands ever more desirable `[RA25]`
> the exceptional desirability of our Maisons, whose products transcend time `[LAA Aug2019]`

**anti-application (limitation):** "desirability" is also the story a chairman must tell; the word ≠ proof a brand is actually desirable (Tension A).

### H2 — Look beyond the figures; think in decades, not quarters `[L0319][RA25]`

> To understand LVMH, you have to look beyond the figures `[L0319]`
> LVMH is also a family group which, by definition and by choice, has a long-term vision `[RA25]`

**anti-application (blind spot):** a family-controlled group can afford patience; not every business has that structural luxury.

### H3 — Prize the eternal Maison — timelessness + legacy `[L0319]`

> There is something eternal about all our Maisons `[L0319]`
> They draw on their impressive legacy to continue to build their place in history `[L0319]`

**anti-application (limitation):** "eternal" is retrospective, told from the summit; most brands don't become timeless, and legacy can also calcify.

### H4 — Run quality × creativity through decentralized, entrepreneurial Maisons `[L0319][LAA]`

> Our decentralized organization means that we can attract and retain the best talent `[L0319]`
> LVMH is driven by a permanent commitment to perfection and quality `[LAA Feb2020]`

**anti-application (blind spot):** decentralization + star designers works at LVMH's scale/cash; it is not a universal operating model.

### H5 — Honor the past AND invent the future `[L0319][RA24]`

> But the dreams that LVMH inspires do not only pay tribute to the past `[L0319]`
> Preparing for the future means remaining alert and harnessing the key trends of our time `[RA24]`

**anti-application (limitation):** "tradition + modernity" is easy to assert; the discipline is doing both without diluting either.

### H6 — Turn margins into investment capacity; cash flow is opportunity `[RA25][RA24]`

> The Group effectively manages its margins and generates exceptional investment capacity `[RA25]`
> This cash flow is both an outcome and an opportunity `[RA24]`

**anti-application (blind spot):** cash-flow-funded expansion is the consolidator's advantage; for a single brand, the same logic can justify over-acquisition.
