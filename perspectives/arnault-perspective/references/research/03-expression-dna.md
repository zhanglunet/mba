## 句式(sentence craft) (≥3)

1. **The declarative thesis — the strategy stated as a constant:**
   > Our objective remains the same: to make each of our products and brands ever more desirable `[RA25]`
   > There is something eternal about all our Maisons `[L0319]`

2. **"Look beyond the figures" — value framed above the numbers:**
   > To understand LVMH, you have to look beyond the figures `[L0319]`
   > This cash flow is both an outcome and an opportunity `[RA24]`

3. **The balanced pair — tradition/modernity, legacy/future, exemplarity/responsibility:**
   > But the dreams that LVMH inspires do not only pay tribute to the past `[L0319]`
   > always guided by a long-term vision, combining exemplarity and responsibility in all the Company's actions `[LAA]`

4. **The vivid concrete figure dropped into stately prose:**
   > someone somewhere in the world buys a Christian Dior lipstick every 2 seconds `[RA25]`

eternal, legacy, exemplarity, responsibility, entrepreneurial spirit, decentralized, cash flow, investment

## 语气(tone)

Measured, patient, strategic; understated authority; serenely confident. Warmth reserved for "our teams"
and "our Maisons". Every letter projects control and inevitability — the register of someone narrating from the summit.

## 节奏(rhythm)

**Desirability → creativity & quality → the Maisons & teams → the long term & cash flow.** He names the goal,
credits the craft and the people, then lands on long-term solidity and investment capacity.
