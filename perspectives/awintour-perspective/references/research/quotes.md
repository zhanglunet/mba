## Timelessness & clarity of purpose over the whims of fashion `[Armani]`

- his work neatly evaded the whims and caprices of fashion, achieving a kind of timelessness and clarity of purpose `[Armani]`
- like Coco Chanel, he was only ever interested in treating women with utmost respect `[Armani]`
- that fashion was forever changed by him for the better, and that so many of us owe him so much `[Armani]`

## Power, attitude & making people more themselves `[Armani]`

- always looked elegant and modern and formidable—because he understood power and attitude as well as anyone `[Armani]`
- He made actors look like movie stars, and also somehow more like themselves `[Armani]`
- understanding fashion cannot, should not, be siloed `[Armani]`

## Self-possession — know who you are `[ALT]`

- it was, for André, an act of supreme confidence, of total self-possession. André knew who he was `[ALT]`

## Backwards and forwards at once — honor the past to move forward `[Karl][EL]`

- Backwards and forwards at once: That was Karl's restless way `[Karl]`
- unforgettable fashion, high drama, a sense of the past and the future colliding `[Karl]`
- an ever-turning, ever-changing line captures more energy and life than a straight one `[Karl]`（她引 Hogarth,作 Karl 的隐喻)
- I'm of the great belief that we can find our way forward by also considering the past `[EL]`

## Change as catalyst — try, learn, keep going `[EL]`

- This emergency is a catalyst. We can upend the old ways of working and make fashion new `[EL]`
- Fashion has talked about change for years and now it finally is changing `[EL]`
- Some new ideas and experiments might not work but that's fine. It's important to try and learn and keep trying again `[EL]`
- So, we must change as we re-emerge. We must adapt. We have to `[EL]`
- How will new collections emerge? How will they be shown? Not in the old ways, and I for one am excited about that `[EL]`
- we should produce less—but what we produce must be more valuable and meaningful to those who want it `[EL]`
- It's a brave act to optimistically consider the future—but that's the kind of bravery we need now more than ever `[EL]`

## Champion the new — community, not elitism `[EL]`

- Fashion is too often criticized for being elitist. We know better `[EL]`
- we should come back with purpose: stronger and more thoughtful and more sustainable in what we do `[EL]`
- We need to talk frankly and openly about what fashion is going through, but just as importantly, we need to start imagining what comes next `[EL]`
- It reminds you that our fashion community is exactly that `[EL]`（—a community of people)
- What do we value? What do we truly need? `[EL]`

## Irreplaceability & singular greatness `[Federer]`

- It's not a question of who will be the next Roger Federer: There is, and there will always ever be, just one `[Federer]`
- Some people are as irreplaceable in life as they are in sports `[Federer]`
- You could tell he was special `[Federer]`
- And there was the way he made it all look devastatingly easy `[Federer]`
- he is also able to extend an empathetic attention entirely outside himself `[Federer]`
- He gives to the extent he has received `[Federer]`

## Character & decency in leadership `[Newhouse][EL]`

- His mix of humility and strength, his willingness to step aside while also being at hand for help, is rare in leadership `[Newhouse]`
- He needed no excuse for hope `[Newhouse]`
- We must choose new leadership `[EL]`（政治语境,see 04 caveat)

## Decisiveness in action (verified, pandemic register) `[EL]`

- We must, right this moment, pledge to stay in our homes as much as we possibly can `[EL]`
- For the rest of us all across America, there is no debate or discussion. We must stay in our homes `[EL]`
