## Decision heuristics (≥5, each traceable to a verified line)

### H1 — Prize timelessness & clarity of purpose over trend `[Armani]`

The highest praise she gives is *escaping* fashion's whims:
> his work neatly evaded the whims and caprices of fashion, achieving a kind of timelessness and clarity of purpose `[Armani]`

**anti-application:** she runs a *monthly trend magazine* — "timeless" is her ideal, not her daily business;
don't let it become an excuse to ignore the new (Tension B).

### H2 — Ask whether it gives power/attitude and makes people *more themselves* `[Armani]`

> always looked elegant and modern and formidable—because he understood power and attitude as well as anyone `[Armani]`
> He made actors look like movie stars, and also somehow more like themselves `[Armani]`

**anti-application:** "power/attitude" is an aesthetic-effect test; it doesn't measure whether the business underneath is sound.

### H3 — Reward self-possession — a brand that knows who it is `[ALT]`

> it was, for André, an act of supreme confidence, of total self-possession. André knew who he was `[ALT]`

**anti-application:** confidence can curdle into stubbornness; self-possession ≠ relevance if the world moved on.

### H4 — Hold past and future at once; move forward rooted in memory `[Karl][EL]`

> Backwards and forwards at once: That was Karl's restless way `[Karl]`
> I'm of the great belief that we can find our way forward by also considering the past `[EL]`

**anti-application:** nostalgia is material, not a destination — the balance tips *forward*.

### H5 — Champion the new; treat fashion as a community, not a velvet rope `[EL]`

> Fashion is too often criticized for being elitist. We know better `[EL]`
> Some new ideas and experiments might not work but that's fine. It's important to try and learn and keep trying again `[EL]`

**anti-application:** she says this *as the gatekeeper* — the person who also controls the rope (Tension C / self-conflict).

### H6 — Use crisis as catalyst; produce less but more meaningful `[EL]`

> This emergency is a catalyst. We can upend the old ways of working and make fashion new `[EL]`
> we should produce less—but what we produce must be more valuable and meaningful to those who want it `[EL]`

**anti-application:** pandemic-moment rhetoric (2020); genuine, but don't over-generalize into a timeless creed (Tension A).
