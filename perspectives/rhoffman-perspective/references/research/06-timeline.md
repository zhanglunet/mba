## D. Anti-Fabrication Notes

- **AI-era essay/speech dates (§C) are primary and exact** (from the source and
  the source manifest, extracted this pass).
- **Career/biographical dates (§A–B) are secondary** (en.wikipedia.org), reliable
  for years but **not corpus-verified**; the two Stanford/Oxford nodes are
  cross-confirmed by first-party lines in the 2024 speeches.
- The **LinkedIn→Microsoft deal figure, Greylock fund figures, and any valuation/
  return** are NOT asserted — flag as *requires live verification*.
- Books before 2023 (*Start-up of You*, *The Alliance*, *Blitzscaling*) are listed
  as biographical facts but their contents are **attributed, not quoted** (outside
  the readable corpus).
